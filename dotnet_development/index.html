Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our digital marketing services and approach.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results vary by channel and strategy. PPC campaigns can show immediate results, while SEO typically takes 3-6 months. Social media and content marketing usually show significant results within 2-4 months. We provide regular reports to track progress and optimize performance.

What's included in your digital marketing packages?

Our packages are customized based on your needs but typically include strategy development, campaign setup and management, content creation, performance monitoring, and monthly reporting. We also provide dedicated account management and ongoing optimization.

How do you measure marketing success and ROI?

We track key performance indicators relevant to your goals: website traffic, lead generation, conversion rates, cost per acquisition, and revenue attribution. We use advanced analytics tools and provide detailed monthly reports with actionable insights.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

We have experience across multiple industries including e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, technology, finance, education, and more. Our team adapts strategies to meet industry-specific requirements and compliance standards.

What's your approach to budget allocation across channels?

We analyze your business goals, target audience, and competitive landscape to recommend optimal budget allocation. We typically start with proven channels for your industry, then expand based on performance data and results.
